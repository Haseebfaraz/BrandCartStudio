AU LeatherX is an eCommerce store specializing in premium jackets, facing certain issues that were affecting its sales and retention rates. They wanted to boost their conversion rate and retain customers for their Shopify website. With our assistance, they were able to resolve some issues, including cart abandonment and retain customers.

Challenges We Face

AU LeatherX encountered the following hurdles in their customer journey

High Cart
Abandonment

A significant percentage of customers abandoned their carts before completing purchases.

Slow Checkout
Experience

The checkout process was cumbersome, leading to frustration and loss of potential sales.

Low Repeat
Purchase Rate

The store struggled with retaining customers, resulting in a low rate of repeat purchases.

Limited Upsell
Opportunities

There was minimal use of upselling or cross-selling strategies to increase average order value.

Solutions

Our Approach to
Solving Complex
Challenges

We devised a multi-faceted approach to address these challenges

Checkout Optimization
  • Streamlined checkout for fewer steps.
  • Added multiple payment options for flexibility.
  • Reduced friction on mobile checkout.

Result: Faster, smoother purchase experience.

Mobile Optimization
  • Improved mobile UI/UX for better navigation.
  • Optimized page speed for faster loading.
  • Enhanced product page layout for conversions.

Result: Increased engagement from mobile users.

Email Marketing Automation
  • Cart abandonment recovery emails.
  • Post-purchase follow-up sequences.
  • Personalized product recommendations.

Result: Higher recovery and repeat purchase rates.
