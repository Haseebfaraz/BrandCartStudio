Medicure Billing, a clinical billing platform, required a new and updated website and also a better lead generation system. Their existing setup was not supporting growth, and they were losing opportunities to connect with their potential clients. The goal was to help them develop a new website as well as an effective system of generating leads.

Key Challenges 

Medicure Billing faced several key issues that limited their growth

Outdated
Website

The existing website lacked a modern structure and a user-friendly experience.

Low Lead
Generation

There was no clear system in place to capture and convert potential clients.

Poor Search
Rankings

The site struggled to appear in search results for relevant healthcare keywords.

No Email
Strategy

There was no structured approach to nurture or engage leads through email.

Solutions

Our Approach to
Solving Complex
Challenges

We implemented a targeted strategy to increase visibility, engagement, and lead generation

Custom Website Development
  • Built a modern, user-friendly website from scratch.
  • Improved navigation and overall user experience.
  • Designed clear pathways for lead conversion.

Result: Better user experience and higher engagement

SEO Optimization
  • Targeted healthcare-specific keywords.
  • Improved site structure and technical SEO.
  • Optimized pages for better search visibility.

Result: Increased organic traffic and rankings

Lead-Focused Landing Pages
  • Created dedicated landing pages for services.
  • Designed conversion-driven layouts.
  • Added clear call-to-action elements.

Result: Higher lead capture and conversions.

Email Marketing Campaigns
  • Set up automated email sequences.
  • Nurtured leads through targeted messaging.
  • Improved engagement with consistent follow-ups.

Result: Stronger lead nurturing and engagement.
