Genuine Jacket Store, an eCommerce brand offering stylish outerwear, aimed to expand internationally and strengthen its online presence. However, limited visibility and performance challenges were holding growth back. However, they were facing challenges in getting more visibility and performance. Our strategy was to increase traffic, global presence, and user experience for scalable growth.

The Challenges Encountered

Genuine Jacket Store faced several key obstacles in scaling its business

Low
Organic Traffic

The website lacked strong search visibility, limiting consistent inbound traffic

Limited
International Sales

Global customers faced friction due to currency and shipping limitations.

Poor
Mobile Experience

The mobile interface was not optimized, leading to lower engagement and conversions.

Weak Social Media
Performance

Social platforms were underutilized, resulting in low traffic and minimal revenue contribution.

Solutions

Our Approach to
Solving Complex
Challenges

We implemented a full-funnel growth strategy focused on visibility, global access, and performance

Global Store Optimization
  • Enabled multi-currency functionality for international buyers.
  • Integrated global shipping solutions.
  • Simplified checkout for international customers.

Result: Increased accessibility for global customers.

SEO & Category Optimization
  • Performed technical SEO fixes to improve site structure.
  • Optimized product and category pages for search intent.
  • Improved internal linking and keyword targeting.

Result: Higher rankings and organic visibility

Mobile-First Redesign
  • Redesigned mobile UI for better navigation.
  • Improved page speed and responsiveness.
  • Optimized mobile product pages for conversions.

Result: Better mobile engagement and conversions

Social Media Marketing
  • Launched targeted ad campaigns across platforms.
  • Created engaging creatives for audience targeting.
  • Focused on conversion-driven social funnels.

Result: Increased traffic and revenue from social channels
